Notes and Rules for Mandrake Draught

Mandrake Draught requires the clause of “Once per turn” on Characters to use. Currently this card works with Arthur Weasley, Colin Creevey, Millicent Bulstrode, Professor Remus Lupin and Stan Shunpike.
